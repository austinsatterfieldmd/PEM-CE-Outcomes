## The 3 Stories

### Story 1: Hematologist-Oncologists
**Finding:** No overall academic-community difference on prescribing topics, but CAR-T shows academic advantage (+10.3pp)

### Story 2: APPs
**Finding:** Started 8.6pp behind Hem-Onc (p<0.001), narrowed to 5.9pp after education—CME effectively upskills the team

### Story 3: Nurses
**Finding:** 4.2pp greater improvement on role-relevant supportive care topics; shared decision-making showed +24.4pp gain

### Longitudinal
**Finding:** Consistent 17-20pp improvement across all 4 years; 2025 shows audience convergence

## How to Calculate Scores

```
Pre-test % = (PRESCORECALC / PRESCOREN) × 100
Post-test % = (POSTSCORECALC / POSTSCOREN) × 100
Improvement = Post-test % - Pre-test %
```

**Important:** Always aggregate sums before dividing (not average of percentages)
