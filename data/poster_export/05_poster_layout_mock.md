## Recommended Visualizations

### Story 1: Academic vs Community (Center Column - Top)
**Chart Type:** Grouped bar chart
- X-axis: Drug Class (CAR-T, Bispecific, ADC)
- Y-axis: Post-test score (%)
- Bars: Academic (dark blue) vs Community (teal)
- Highlight: CAR-T shows academic advantage

### Story 2: APPs Closing the Gap (Center Column - Middle)
**Chart Type:** Side-by-side bar comparison with annotation
- Two groups: APPs vs Hem-Onc
- Each group: Pre-test (light) and Post-test (dark) bars
- Arrow showing gap narrowing from 8.6pp → 5.9pp
- p-value annotation

### Story 3: Nurses by Topic (Center Column - Bottom)
**Chart Type:** Horizontal bar chart
- Y-axis: Topics (Shared decision-making, AE management, Safety profile)
- X-axis: Score (%)
- Each bar shows Pre (light) → Post (dark)
- Highlight largest gains

### Longitudinal Trends (Right Column - Top)
**Chart Type:** Multi-line graph
- X-axis: Year (2022, 2023, 2024, 2025)
- Y-axis: Post-test score (%)
- 3 lines: Hem-Onc, APPs, Nurses
- Shows convergence in 2025

---

## Key Design Principles

1. **Visual hierarchy**: 3 stories dominate center column
2. **Consistent color coding**: Same colors for each audience throughout
3. **Key findings boxes**: Highlight takeaways below each chart
4. **Scale prominence**: Make 10,357 responses visible early
5. **White space**: Don't overcrowd—let data breathe
6. **Statistical annotations**: Include p-values where significant
