## STORY 2: APPs — CLOSING THE GAP
### All Topics

### Overall Performance

| Metric | APPs (n=650) | Hem-Onc (n=764) |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| Pre-test | 42.5% | 51.1% |
| Post-test | 62.8% | 68.7% |

### Gap Analysis

| Gap | Difference | p-value | Interpretation |
|-----|------------|---------|----------------|
| Pre-test | 8.6pp behind | **p=0.0001** | Highly significant gap |
| Post-test | 5.9pp behind | p=0.019 | Gap narrowed by 31% |

### By Topic Type

| Topic Type | Pre | Post | Gain | n |
|------------|-----|------|------|---|
| Prescribing topics | 45.3% | 65.1% | +19.8pp | 312 |
| Supportive topics | 39.9% | 60.7% | +20.7pp | 338 |

### Key Finding
- APPs started significantly behind Hem-Onc (8.6pp gap, p<0.001)
- APPs showed **LARGER improvement** (+20.3pp vs +17.6pp)
- Gap narrowed from 8.6pp to 5.9pp after education
- Demonstrates CME effectively upskills the multidisciplinary team
