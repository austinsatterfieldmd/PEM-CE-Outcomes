## CMML Overview

**Definition:** Myelodysplastic/myeloproliferative neoplasm (MDS/MPN overlap)

**Key features:**
- Persistent monocytosis (≥1 × 10⁹/L, ≥10% of WBC)
- Dysplasia in one or more myeloid lineages
- Can have proliferative or dysplastic phenotype

**Classification:**
- CMML-0, CMML-1, CMML-2 (by blast percentage)
- Proliferative (WBC ≥13K) vs Dysplastic (WBC <13K)
