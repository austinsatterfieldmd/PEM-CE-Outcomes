## NF1 Plexiform Neurofibroma Overview

**Definition:** Benign but debilitating tumors arising in patients with Neurofibromatosis type 1 (NF1)

**Key features:**
- RAS/MAPK pathway dysregulation (NF1 loss → increased RAS signaling)
- Plexiform neurofibromas can cause significant morbidity
- Risk of malignant transformation to MPNST
