## PTCL Overview

**PTCL subtypes:**
- PTCL-NOS (not otherwise specified) - most common
- Angioimmunoblastic T-cell lymphoma (AITL)
- Anaplastic large cell lymphoma (ALCL) - ALK+ vs ALK-
- Adult T-cell leukemia/lymphoma (ATLL)
- Extranodal NK/T-cell lymphoma, nasal type
- Hepatosplenic T-cell lymphoma

**Key difference from B-cell lymphomas:** No rituximab, different drug landscape
