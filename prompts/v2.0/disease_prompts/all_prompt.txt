- **AE management** = knowing WHAT TO DO after an AE has already occurred (intervention, dose modification)

- "What is the mechanism of asparaginase?" → **Mechanism of action**
- "What does MRD status mean for prognosis?" → **Prognosis** or **Biomarker testing**

### Context-Based Inference for Asparaginase

**Infer Erwinia formulation when:**
- Question discusses "hypersensitivity" or "allergic reaction" to E. coli-derived asparaginase

**Infer Calaspargase pegol (Asparlas) when:**
- Question mentions "longer half-life" or "extended interval dosing"

**Infer Pegaspargase when:**
- Standard pediatric-inspired regimen without specific formulation named
- First-line asparaginase without hypersensitivity context
- Most induction protocols default to pegaspargase

**Tag generic "Asparaginase" when:**
- Generic discussion of asparaginase mechanism, monitoring, or toxicity
- No context to infer specific formulation
- Historical or general discussion
